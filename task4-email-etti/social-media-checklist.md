# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

## Do’s
- Use respectful and professional language online.
- Think before posting or commenting.
- Protect your personal information and privacy.
- Verify information before sharing it.
- Use social media to build knowledge and positive connections.

## Don’ts
- Do not post offensive, harmful, or abusive content.
- Do not share fake news or unverified information.
- Do not reveal passwords, OTPs, or sensitive details.
- Do not overshare private life publicly.
- Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments unnecessarily.
