# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share OTPs, UPI PINs, passwords, or bank details with anyone.
- Do not scan QR codes sent by unknown people to receive money.
- Always verify payment requests before approving them.
- Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication wherever possible.
- Avoid clicking suspicious links received through SMS, email, or WhatsApp.
- Check the sender’s identity before responding to urgent financial messages.
- Keep your apps and phone software updated regularly.
- Use only official banking and payment apps downloaded from trusted sources.
- Be extra careful while using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions.
- If fraud happens, report it immediately on the National Cyber Crime Portal: https://www.cybercrime.gov.in
- You can also call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930
